Gaussin Normal Distribution: Bell curve.  
- Center: mean.  
- Step/ one unit: standard deviation  
  
- 1 sd - (-1 sd): 68%  
- 2 sd - (-2 sd): 95%  
- 3 sd - (-3 sd): 97%  
  
X axis: random variables (Height of students)  
Y axis: Probability density function.
